The LinkedIn Glow-Up

Transform a "meh" profile into a recruiter magnet. Live makeover.

This session focuses on optimizing your LinkedIn profile to powerfully showcase your expertise and attract opportunities, making it a true digital representation of your **authority**.

Speech Outline (10 Minutes - AIDA Model):
  • **Attention (1.5 min):** Hook with the relatable problem of a overlooked LinkedIn profile.
    • "Is your LinkedIn a beacon of brilliance, or a digital tumbleweed?"
  • **Interest (3 min):** Explain the power of an optimized LinkedIn and its key components.
    • "More than a job board: your 24/7 spokesperson."
    • Briefly explain headline, about, experience, skills, recommendations.
  • **Desire (3 min):** Paint a picture of success and opportunity.
    • "Imagine recruiters seeking *you*, collaborators reaching out."
    • Share an anonymized "before/after" impact example.
  • **Action (2.5 min):** Provide immediate, actionable steps.
    • "Headline audit, About section hook, action verbs & numbers in experience."
    • Call to Action: "Dedicate 15 minutes this week. Your future self will thank you."
5-Minute Activity: Profile Power Scan
  • **Objective:** Quickly identify immediate improvements on your own LinkedIn profile.
  • **How:** Speaker provides rapid-fire prompts; participants self-assess and share one "aha!" moment in chat.

Power Pose Rhetoric

Body language that screams "I run things." Strike a pose, get feedback.

Discover how intentional body language can dramatically enhance your confidence and perceived **authority**, particularly in virtual interactions.

Speech Outline (10 Minutes - AIDA Model):
  • **Attention (1.5 min):** Hook with the unspoken language of presence.
    • "Do your non-verbal cues affirm your **authority**, or undermine it?"
    • Introduce "Power Pose Rhetoric" concept.
  • **Interest (3 min):** Explain the science behind power posing and virtual adaptations.
    • Amy Cuddy's research: physiology changes.
    • Focus on shoulders, chest, head, eye contact, groundedness, breathing.
  • **Desire (3 min):** Envision commanding a room with silent communication.
    • "Imagine delivering a presentation where every gesture reinforces your message."
    • Real-world impact: job interviews, pitches, meetings.
  • **Action (2.5 min):** Demonstrate and prompt practice of virtual power poses.
    • "The Wonder Woman/Superman (seated), Grounded Lean, Open Embrace."
    • Call to Action: "Consciously adopt a pose in one meeting this week. Observe impact."
5-Minute Activity: Virtual Confidence Stance
  • **Objective:** Silently practice power poses adapted for virtual presence.
  • **How:** Follow speaker's demonstration; share one word in chat describing how the pose felt.

The Elevator Pitch Seduction

A 30-second spiel that lands deals (or dates). Speed-dating style drills.

Learn to craft a concise, compelling, and memorable pitch that instantly communicates your value and **authority**, seizing every unexpected opportunity.

Speech Outline (10 Minutes - AIDA Model):
  • **Attention (1.5 min):** Hook with the missed opportunity of a weak introduction.
    • "30 seconds with a CEO â€“ do you captivate or stumble?"
    • Introduce "Elevator Pitch Seduction."
  • **Interest (3 min):** Explain the broad utility of an elevator pitch and its components.
    • Beyond elevators: networking, bios, impromptu meetings.
    • Three Pillars: Problem, Solution, Impact/Benefit.
  • **Desire (3 min):** Visualize confident, intriguing introductions.
    • "Imagine seeing heads nod, hearing 'Tell me more!'"
    • Impact on career/networking: position yourself as an expert with **authority**.
    • Example: Jane's "designer" vs. "brand storyteller" pitch.
  • **Action (2.5 min):** Guide participants to build their core pitch.
    • "Identify your problem, unique solution, quantifiable benefit."
    • Call to Action: "Write it down, practice daily, deliver to 3 people this week."
5-Minute Activity: Rapid-Fire Pitch Practice
  • **Objective:** Deliver and receive immediate feedback on your elevator pitch in small breakout rooms.
  • **How:** Quick peer practice sessions focusing on clarity and impact.

Speech Outline (10 Minutes - AIDA Model):
  • **Attention (1.5 min):** Hook with a comparison to famous brands.

    • Introduce "Brand or Bland?" concept.
  • **Interest (3 min):** Define personal branding and highlight the cost of blandness.
    • "Sum total of how others perceive you."
    • Missed opportunities, less recognition.
    • Key Elements: Authenticity, Clarity, Consistency, Value Proposition, Visibility.
  • **Desire (3 min):** Envision being the "hire me!" candidate.

  • **Action (2.5 min):** Guide a personal brand audit.

5-Minute Activity: Spot the Spark
  • **Objective:** Analyze anonymized bios and identify elements of strong personal branding/authority.
  • **How:** Quick poll/chat discussion on which bios best exude "Authority" and why.
